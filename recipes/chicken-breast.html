Pan Fried Chicken Breast

Description:

First, brine your chicken breast by adding some salt to it. Allow to rest at room temperature for about 20 to 30 minutes. Heat up your pan on medium and add some oil. When the oil is shimmering hot, add your chicken breast to the pan. Cook for about eight to ten minutes on each side. Add some butter at the end (optional).

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Brine your chicken breast with salt for 20 to 30 minutes at room temperature.
  2. Heat up oil in the pan.
  3. Cook the chicken breast for about eight to ten minutes on each side.
  4. Add some butter at the end (optional).
