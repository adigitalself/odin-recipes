Simple Egg Fried Rice

Description:

First, prepare your ingredients. You will need some scallions or onions, eggs, cooked rice, and some oyster sauce or soy sauce. Clean and dice the scallions. Next, heat up your pan and then add some oil. When the oil is hot and shimmering, add the diced scallions. Add the eggs and scramble it. When the eggs are no longer wet, add the rice and some more oil. Mix everything well. Add the oyster sauce and/or soy sauce. Mix well. Sprinkle some sesame oil or sesame seeds at the end and enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Rice
  • Scallions
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare eggs, scallions, and cooked rice.
  2. Heat up oil in your pan.
  3. Add your diced scallions to the oil and allow to soften.
  4. Add the eggs and scramble.
  5. When the eggs are relatively cooked, add the rice. Mix. Add more oil if needed.
  6. Add the oyster sauce or soy sauce. Mix well.
  7. Sprinkle sesame seed oil or sesame seeds at the end before serving!
